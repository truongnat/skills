# Modeling and strategy selection

- See **problem type taxonomy** in [decision-tree.md](decision-tree.md) (optimization, counting, online, …) after classifying the ask.
- Convert problem text into formal input/output, constraints, and objective.
- Identify structural signals: overlapping subproblems, optimal substructure, monotonicity, sparsity.
- Map to candidate families: greedy, dynamic programming, graph shortest path, union-find, segment tree, search.
- Keep at least one baseline approach for comparison and fallback.
- Choose strategy by asymptotic fit and implementation risk, not habit.

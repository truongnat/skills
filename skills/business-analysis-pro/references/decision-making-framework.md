# Decision-making framework

Move from “we decided” to **reproducible, auditable** choice under constraints.

## Components

1. **Options** — A, B, C (including *do nothing* / *defer* when valid).
2. **Criteria** — E.g. time-to-value, cost, risk, compliance fit, strategic fit, team capacity.
3. **Weights** — Optional: sum to 1.0 per decision; expose weights to sponsors when political sensitivity is high.
4. **Scores** — Normalize where possible (1–5, or qualitative → numeric rubric). Document **who** scored and **when**.
5. **Sensitivity** — If small weight change flips winner → flag **fragile decision**; gather more data or escalate.

## Uncertainty

- **Ranges** instead of point scores when evidence is thin.
- **Confidence** per criterion (high/medium/low).
- **Review trigger** — Revisit decision when assumptions X or Y change.

## Outputs

- **Decision record**: chosen option, **rejected** options with reason, criteria table, date, **approver**.
- Pair **`risk-modeling.md`** for downside of chosen path.

## Anti-pattern

- **Post-hoc rationalization** — Scores invented to match a pre-selected vendor.

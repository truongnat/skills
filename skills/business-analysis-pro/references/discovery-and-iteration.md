# Discovery and iteration

Product discovery is **learn**, not only **document once**.

## Loop

1. **Discover** — Interviews, data, support tickets, **`content-analysis-pro`** on source files.
2. **Hypothesize** — “We believe X will improve metric Y for segment Z.”
3. **Test** — Prototype, pilot, A/B, time-boxed beta — **`testing-pro`** for validation quality.
4. **Learn** — Measure against success criteria; **kill, pivot, or scale**.
5. **Refine** — Update BRD/backlog and **traceability**; **version** the requirement set.

## Artifacts

- **Learning log** alongside **decision log** — what we tried, what failed, **why** we changed direction.

## Boundary

Full **experiment design** (stats power, sample size) may need **`data-analysis-pro`** — **this skill** ensures requirements **stay honest** about what is **proven** vs **assumed**.

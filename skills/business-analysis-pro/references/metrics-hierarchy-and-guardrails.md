# Metrics hierarchy and guardrails

Connect **delivery** metrics to **business outcomes** without metric chaos.

## Hierarchy

1. **North star / outcome** — Primary business or user outcome (revenue, retention, time saved).
2. **Output metrics** — Product behaviors (activation rate, weekly active, completed orders).
3. **Input metrics** — Levers teams pull (onboarding completion, latency, catalog depth).

## Guardrails

- Metrics you **must not** harm while optimizing primary — e.g. **trust**, **support load**, **compliance rate**, **error rate**.

## Conflicts

- **Local vs global optimum** — Team A metric hurts Team B — escalate to **outcome** level or **portfolio** (**`strategic-consulting-pro`**).
- **Vanity metrics** — Clicks without conversion; session time without quality — challenge in review.

## Baseline and targets

- **Baseline** + **target** + **date**; **leading** vs **lagging** called out.

## Pairing

- **`data-analysis-pro`** for definitions, cohorts, and statistical nuance — **this skill** locks **requirements** and **reporting** to agreed definitions.

# Product thinking and outcomes

Bridge **requirements** (what we build) and **outcomes** (what must improve).

## Core concepts

- **Problem / opportunity** — Who hurts, how often, cost of inaction?
- **User / customer segments** — Different jobs-to-be-done; avoid “average user.”
- **Value proposition** — Why this solution vs alternatives (including spreadsheet + manual process).
- **Product–market fit signals** — Retention, expansion, willingness to pay — frame as **hypotheses** if unmeasured.

## Strategy hooks

- **North Star / outcome metric** — One primary **behavioral or business** outcome (not vanity clicks alone).
- **Inputs and leading indicators** — Activation, frequency, funnel conversion — tie to **`metrics-hierarchy-and-guardrails.md`**.
- **Growth vs retention** — Explicit which phase the initiative serves; conflicting tactics called out.

## Business impact (lightweight)

- **Qualitative**: revenue protection, cost avoidance, regulatory avoidance.
- **Quantitative when possible**: ranges, not false precision — source (**finance**, **data-analysis-pro**).

## Boundary

Full **GTM strategy** or **pricing science** may need **`strategic-consulting-pro`** or dedicated finance — **this skill** aligns requirements to **stated** product goals and measurable success.

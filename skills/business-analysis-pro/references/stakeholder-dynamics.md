# Stakeholder dynamics

Lists are not enough — map **power**, **interest**, and **decision rights**.

## Power / interest grids

- **High power, high interest** — Manage closely; early alignment.
- **Low power, high interest** — Keep informed; capture feedback to avoid surprise veto.

## Decision authority

- **RACI** alone does not show **who can override** — document **approval limits** (budget, legal, brand).

## Conflict

- **Same facts, different goals** — Surface **trade-off** explicitly in **`decision-making-framework.md`**.
- **Silent veto** — Late “no” from unconsulted party — mitigate with **`integration-map`** workshops and written RFC for cross-team changes.

## Escalation

- **Trigger**: deadlock, SLA breach on decision, regulatory ambiguity.
- **Path**: sponsor → steering → exec — **named levels**, not “we’ll escalate if needed.”

## Political override

When **HiPPO** wins against analysis — still log **criteria** and **dissenting view** for audit (“informed acceptance of risk”).

# Decision framework and trade-offs

## System maturity vs velocity

| Stage | Emphasis |
|-------|----------|
| **0–1 product** | Few tokens + documentation light; avoid blocking shipping |
| **Scale-up** | Full semantic layers; governance; CI checks |
| **Enterprise** | Multi-brand / white-label — theme variants as first-class |

## Build vs buy

| Choice | Upside | Downside |
|--------|--------|----------|
| **Adopt major kit** (MUI, Radix+Tailwind recipes) | Speed | Visual sameness; escape hatches needed |
| **Headless + custom skin** | Brand control | You own a11y behaviors |
| **Fully custom** | Unique | Highest a11y and maintenance cost |

## Density × audience

| Combo | Stance |
|-------|--------|
| **Prosumer analytics** | Compact tables; monospace/tabular nums — **`foundations-baseline-and-data-ui.md`** |
| **Consumer marketing** | Expressive type; fewer simultaneous metrics — **`decision-tree.md`** |

## Trend adoption

- **Judgment-first** — Clarity and a11y beat novelty — **`trends-themes-and-visual-language.md`**.

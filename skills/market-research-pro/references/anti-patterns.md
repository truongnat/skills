# Market research — anti-patterns

## Single vendor report as fact

- Incentivized narrative.
- **Fix:** Triangulate; separate fact from opinion.

## Spurious precision

- TAM to the dollar from guesses.
- **Fix:** Ranges; scenario table.

## Ignoring substitution

- “No competitors” when substitutes exist.
- **Fix:** Widen category definition.

## Stale data

- Pre-COVID travel stats for 2026 plan without flag.
- **Fix:** Date every statistic.

## Evidence discipline

- Cite **source + date + geography** for non-obvious numbers — **`web-research-pro`**.

---

## No fabricated market sizes

- If only one weak source exists, label **low confidence** — do not round to slick integers without basis.

---

## Conflicting sources

- Present **spread** and what would resolve conflict (better proxy, primary data).

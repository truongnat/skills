# Segment prioritization and GTM hypotheses

## ICP scoring

- Score segments on pain intensity, budget fit, accessibility, and implementation complexity.
- Add strategic fit: referenceability, expansion potential, and retention risk.

## Prioritization model

- Use a simple weighted matrix (attractiveness x feasibility).
- Show trade-offs explicitly rather than averaging away hard constraints.

## GTM hypothesis design

- Channel hypothesis (PLG, partner, outbound, community).
- Messaging hypothesis by segment (economic buyer vs operator).
- Conversion checkpoints and kill criteria.

## Decision output

- Recommend primary and secondary segment with rationale.
- Include "what would change the decision" triggers.

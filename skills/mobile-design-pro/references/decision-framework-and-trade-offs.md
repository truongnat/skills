# Decision framework and trade-offs

## Visual parity vs native excellence

| Stance | When |
|--------|------|
| **Strong brand parity** | Marketing-heavy apps; accept extra engineering |
| **Platform-first UX** | Productivity / OS-conforming expectations — fewer surprises |

## Information density

| Density | Risk |
|---------|------|
| **Compact** | Faster scan; tap errors |
| **Generous** | Safer touch; more scroll |

## Bottom vs top primary action

| Choice | Trade-off |
|--------|-----------|
| **Bottom primary** | Thumb-friendly; may fight platform toolbar patterns |
| **Top primary** | Familiar for “done/save” on iOS sometimes — validate reach — **`decision-tree.md`** |

## Motion and haptics

| Lever | Guidance |
|-------|-----------|
| **Animation** | Reinforce state; never sole channel for critical info |
| **Haptics** | Sparingly; respect reduce motion + battery — **`tips-and-tricks.md`** |

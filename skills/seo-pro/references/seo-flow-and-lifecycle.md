# SEO flow and lifecycle

End-to-end **program** — not a one-time meta tag fix. Align with business goals and measurable outcomes.

## Phases (iterative)

1. **Discover & baseline** — Business goals (traffic, leads, brand queries). Current **Search Console** / analytics baseline; competitor and query landscape.
2. **Audit** — Technical crawl health, index coverage, on-page templates, content gaps, **Core Web Vitals**, mobile UX.
3. **Strategy** — Priority URLs, topic clusters, **intent** mapping (informational vs transactional), locale/market plan.
4. **Implement** — Technical fixes (speed, crawl budget, structured data), template-level on-page rules, internal linking, content calendar.
5. **Measure** — Rankings (tool + GSC), impressions/clicks, conversions attributed to organic; segment branded vs non-branded.
6. **Iterate** — Hypothesis-driven changes; avoid changing too many variables at once; document what shipped when.

## Roles (typical)

- **Product/engineering** — Routing, SSR/SSG, headers, redirects, JS delivery.
- **Content** — Quality, E-E-A-T signals, freshness where relevant.
- **SEO specialist** — Rules, prioritization, GSC interpretation.

## KPIs (pick a few)

- **Organic sessions** / **conversions** (not only position #1 vanity).
- **Impressions vs CTR** — diagnose title/meta vs demand.
- **Index coverage** — valid vs error/excluded in GSC.

## Official orientation

- [Google Search Central](https://developers.google.com/search/docs) — how Google crawls, indexes, ranks (guidelines, not guarantees).
- [Bing Webmaster Guidelines](https://www.bing.com/webmasters/help/webmasters-guidelines-30fba23a) — second large engine.

## Relationship to other skills

- **Rendering** and **metadata** implementation: **`nextjs-pro`** / **`react-pro`**.
- **CDN and caching** that affect TTFB: **`deployment-pro`**.

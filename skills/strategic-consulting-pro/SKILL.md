# Strategic consulting (professional)

Skill text is **English**; answer in the user’s preferred language when rules or the conversation specify it.

Use reputable strategy references and **current** evidence; this skill encodes **decision-driven synthesis**, **trade-off clarity**, and **assumption transparency** — not generic framework dumps. Confirm **decision owner**, **time horizon**, **constraints** (capital, people, regulation), and **success metrics**.

## Boundary

**`strategic-consulting-pro`** owns **strategic problem framing**, **option sets**, **criteria-based comparison**, **scenario and portfolio logic**, and **executive-ready recommendations**. **`business-analysis-pro`** owns **requirements and delivery artifacts**. **`planning-pro`** owns **detailed sequencing and dependency plans** once a strategic choice exists. **`market-research-pro`** owns **primary market evidence gathering**.

## When to use

- Clarify strategic questions before jumping to tactics.
- Compare options on shared criteria with explicit trade-offs.
- Prioritize initiatives under resource constraints.
- Scenario planning under uncertainty.
- Executive memos, decision logs, and kill criteria.

## When not to use

- **Implementation-level** task breakdown without strategic choice — **`planning-pro`** or **`business-analysis-pro`**.
- **Pure market data collection** as the main deliverable — **`market-research-pro`** may lead.
- **Legal, tax, or investment advice** — counsel/finance; this skill flags handoff only.

## Required inputs

- **Decision owner** and **deadline** (or “none” with risk called out).
- **Constraints** (capital, regulatory, capability).
- **Success definition** or metrics when available.

## Expected output

Follow **Suggested response format** strictly — system model through residual risks.

## Workflow

1. Confirm decision context, owner, horizon, constraints, and metrics.
2. Apply summaries; open `references/`; anchor on **`strategic-decision-and-stakeholder-system-model.md`** for governance spine.
3. Respond with **Suggested response format**; include **failure modes** when politics, thin data, or execution gap risks dominate.

## Suggested response format (STRICT — implement / review)

1. **Context** — Owner, horizon, constraints, industry/stage if known.
2. **Problem / goal** — Strategic decision question; what “good” looks like.
3. **System design** — Decision spine, RACI/evidence loop — **`strategic-decision-and-stakeholder-system-model.md`**.
4. **Decision reasoning** — Criteria table; scenarios if high uncertainty — **`decision-framework-and-trade-offs.md`** / **`scenario-planning-and-risk.md`**.
5. **Implementation sketch** — Option matrix, scenario table, or memo outline — still deliverable artifacts (labeled as structured output).
6. **Trade-offs** — Explicit sacrifices (speed, cost, focus, risk).
7. **Failure modes** — Bias, stale data, hidden veto, execution gap — **`failure-modes-detection-mitigation.md`**.
8. **Residual risks** — Monitoring triggers; **`planning-pro`** / **`market-research-pro`** / **`security-pro`** handoffs.

## Quick examples

### 1 — Simple (common)

**Input:** Prioritize enterprise upsell vs SME expansion next two quarters.  
**Expected output:** Full **Suggested response format** — criteria, scenarios, capability fit, sequenced recommendation, confidence — **`prioritization-and-resource-allocation.md`**.

### 2 — Tricky (edge case)

**Input:** CEO wants one slide but team has 20 pages of analysis.  
**Expected output:** One-slide thesis + appendix map; explicit rejected options — **`reporting-and-executive-communication.md`**, **`failure-modes-detection-mitigation.md`** (framework theater avoidance).

### 3 — Cross-skill

**Input:** Build vs buy for auth — then roadmap.  
**Expected output:** This skill for criteria/options — **`auth-pro`** for technical trade-offs — **`planning-pro`** for waves — **`market-research-pro`** if vendor landscape thin.

## Checklist before calling the skill done

### Decision quality

- [ ] Strategic question and **decision owner** explicit — **`quality-validation-and-guardrails.md`**.
- [ ] **Consistent criteria** across options; ≥ **two** credible alternatives — **`failure-modes-detection-mitigation.md`**.
- [ ] Assumptions and **confidence** visible — **`reporting-and-executive-communication.md`**.

### Outcomes

- [ ] Measurable outcomes and **next steps** or **`planning-pro`** handoff — **`strategic-decision-and-stakeholder-system-model.md`**.
- [ ] **`integration-map.md`** used for research, BA, security, or planning depth.
- [ ] No **fabricated** market statistics — **`quality-validation-and-guardrails.md`**.

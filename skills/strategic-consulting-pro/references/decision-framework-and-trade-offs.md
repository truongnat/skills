## Where to play vs how to win

| Lens | Question |
|------|----------|
| **Where to play** | Which segments, geos, customer types? |
| **How to win** | Which capabilities, moats, economics? |

**Trade-off:** broad “where” without “how” → unfunded mandates — pair **`prioritization-and-resource-allocation.md`**.

## Build vs partner vs buy

| Lean **build** | Lean **buy/partner** |
|----------------|----------------------|
| Core differentiator, scarce talent available | Commodity, time-to-market critical, vendor mature |

**Trade-off:** control vs speed/cost — technical depth often needs **`auth-pro`**, **`nestjs-pro`**, or vendor diligence via **`market-research-pro`**.

## Concentration vs diversification

| Concentrate | Diversify |
|-------------|-----------|
| Double down on winning wedge | Reduce correlated downside |

**Trade-off:** revenue focus vs **resilience** — scenario stress — **`scenario-planning-and-risk.md`**.

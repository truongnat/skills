## Process failures

| Failure mode | Detection | Mitigation |
|--------------|-----------|------------|
| **Framework theater** | Deck full of models, no decision | Tie every section to decision criteria — **`problem-framing-and-options.md`** |
| **Single-option advocacy** | “Only path is X” | Force **do nothing** and at least one credible alternative — **`anti-patterns.md`** |
| **Strategy without metrics** | No way to know if winning | North-star + leading indicators — **`reporting-and-executive-communication.md`** |

## Evidence and bias

| Failure mode | Detection | Mitigation |
|--------------|-----------|------------|
| **Confirmation bias** | Evidence only supports pre-bought answer | Red-team option; assign devil’s advocate — **`scenario-planning-and-risk.md`** |
| **HiPPO override without criteria** | Decision reverses with mood | Written criteria + decision log — **`decision-tree.md`** |
| **Stale market claims** | Numbers from old reports | Date-stamp sources; **`web-research-pro`** / **`market-research-pro`** refresh — **`quality-validation-and-guardrails.md`** |

# Prioritization and resource allocation

## Prioritization rubric

- Impact (revenue, risk reduction, strategic leverage).
- Feasibility (capabilities, dependencies, execution complexity).
- Time-to-value (near-term vs long-term).

## Sequencing

- Prefer coherent waves over too many parallel bets.
- Explicitly call out prerequisites and gating dependencies.
- Reserve capacity for risk mitigation and core operations.

## Portfolio balance

- Balance core optimization, adjacent expansion, and innovation bets.
- Show trade-offs when reallocating people or budget.

# Problem framing and strategic options

## Frame the decision

- Define the core decision in one sentence.
- Clarify objective function (growth, margin, resilience, speed).
- State non-negotiable constraints and time horizon.

## Build option quality

- Generate at least 3 credible options (including status quo).
- Avoid false binaries unless constraints force them.
- Separate "what to do" from "how to execute."

## Option filters

- Strategic fit with mission and capability base.
- Economic potential and downside exposure.
- Operational feasibility and change burden.

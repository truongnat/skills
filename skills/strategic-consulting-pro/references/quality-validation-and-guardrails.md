## Evidence and recency

- Tag **confidence** (high/medium/low) and **source date** for market or regulatory claims.
- When data is thin, label **proxy assumptions** and what would falsify them — **`scenario-planning-and-risk.md`**.

---

## Anti-fabrication

- **Do not invent** market sizes, growth rates, competitor moves, or survey results — use ranges or “TBD pending research” with **`market-research-pro`** / **`web-research-pro`**.
- Do not present **opinion** as **measured fact** — separate inference from data.

---

## Legal and fiduciary tone

- This skill is **not** legal, tax, or investment advice — flag when counsel or finance sign-off is required — especially regulated industries (**`security-pro`** for data strategy touchpoints).

---

## Review checklist

- [ ] Decision question, owner, and horizon stated.
- [ ] At least **two** real alternatives plus **do nothing/defer** where sensible.
- [ ] Shared **criteria** table across options.
- [ ] Assumptions and confidence visible — **`reporting-and-executive-communication.md`**.
- [ ] Next-step owners or **`planning-pro`** handoff explicit.

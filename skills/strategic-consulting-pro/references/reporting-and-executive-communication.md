# Reporting and executive communication

## Recommended structure

1. Decision context and objective.
2. Options and evaluation criteria.
3. Recommendation and rationale.
4. Risks, assumptions, and confidence.
5. Immediate next actions and owners.

## Communication style

- Lead with recommendation, then evidence.
- Keep strategy memo concise and decision-oriented.
- Use clear confidence labels (high/medium/low) per major claim.

## Decision log

- Capture decision date, owner, assumptions, and revisit trigger.

# Scenario planning and risk

## Scenario setup

- Build base, upside, and downside scenarios with explicit assumptions.
- Tie each scenario to observable trigger signals.
- Keep assumptions independent where possible to avoid hidden coupling.

## Risk lens

- Strategic risk: wrong market or positioning bet.
- Execution risk: capability, sequencing, and change fatigue.
- External risk: regulation, competition, macro shifts.

## Contingency planning

- Define trigger thresholds for pivot, pause, or accelerate.
- Document mitigation owners and monitoring cadence.

## Decision spine

Effective strategy work answers:

1. **What decision** is required (owner, deadline)?
2. **What options** are in scope (including “do nothing / defer”)?
3. **What criteria** judge options (growth, risk, capital, time)?
4. **What evidence** supports each option (market, capability, regulation)?

Without (1), output becomes **analysis without commitment** — **`failure-modes-detection-mitigation.md`**.

## Stakeholders and governance

| Element | Role |
|---------|------|
| **Decision owner** | Accountable for picking an option |
| **RACI** | Who recommends, consults, informs — reduces political thrash |
| **Decision log** | Records chosen option, rejected alternatives, assumptions |

Misaligned **RACI** produces **re-litigation** after every meeting — **`edge-cases.md`**.

## Evidence loop

```
Hypothesis → gather evidence (market-research / web-research / data-analysis)
  → synthesize options → stress-test assumptions → recommendation
```

Stale or **single-source** evidence inflates confidence — **`quality-validation-and-guardrails.md`**.
